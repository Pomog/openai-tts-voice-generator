Hello capsuleers.

In this video, I will show how I complete Mission 49: Our Man Dagan from the Sisters of EVE epic arc.

This mission is famous among new players because Dagan has a very strong tank.
Many beginner ships can damage him, but they cannot apply enough DPS to finish the job.

Today I am using a Cormorant destroyer with railguns and a light missile launcher.
This is not an expensive fit, around 5.2 million ISK, but it has enough damage to complete the mission if you fly it correctly.

The most important lesson in this mission is simple: your ship fit matters.
You need enough DPS, good ammo, and good range control.
