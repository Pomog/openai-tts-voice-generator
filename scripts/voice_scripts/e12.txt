Before warping to the site, I refit my Loki.

I remove some scanning modules and replace them with passive shield tank modules.

The reason is simple: if I fail a hack in this type of site, I expect an explosion.

Depending on the site and container, this explosion can deal serious damage.

So I want more buffer before I start hacking.

I also replaced my warp core stabilizer with a cargo extender.

And honestly, this is not the smartest decision.

More cargo space is useful, but losing extra escape protection in null sec is dangerous.

This is exactly the kind of small decision that can become expensive later.
