Before I go to the Ghost Site, I want to make an undock bookmark.

This is very important when using stations in dangerous space.

After undocking, I turn on my MWD and continue flying straight away from the station.

I keep the same direction as the ship had after undocking.

Then, when I am far enough away, I create a bookmark.

This gives me a fast escape point.

Later, if I undock and something dangerous is waiting near the station, I can instantly warp to this bookmark instead of slowly aligning.

This is a small preparation step, but it can save the ship.
