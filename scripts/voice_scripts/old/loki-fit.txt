This is my Loki fit for Capsuleer Day XXIII Warpath Intelligence data sites.

It is not a beginner fit, and it is not the cheapest option. I use it because it gives me cloaky travel, good scanning strength, fast movement, and comfortable hacking.

The Loki is expensive for this content, but it is comfortable. You can scan, cloak, hack, and move quickly between systems.

The main risk is not the site itself, but other players.

My basic rule is simple: cloak while travelling, do not sit still after hacking, and always watch local and d-scan.

The Loki helps, but it does not make you invincible. If you are in low-sec, null-sec, or wormholes, assume someone can be hunting you.
