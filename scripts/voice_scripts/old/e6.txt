One of the biggest mistakes in null sec is warping gate to gate without thinking.

You can land in a bubble.

You can land near a camp.

You can be decloaked.

And if you are unlucky, your expensive exploration ship can die very quickly.

So always make safe spots.

Warp between celestials and create bookmarks in deep space.

Use them when you need to scan, wait, refit, or check the next move.

If a system looks dangerous, do not force the route.

You are not there to prove anything.

You are there to bring loot home.

Also, keep local chat visible all the time.

If a new pilot enters the system, treat that as new information.

Pause, check, and decide.
