The first databank shows around 15.1 million ISK estimated value.

The second one shows around 7.75 million ISK.

The third one shows around 6.29 million ISK.

But because I did not tag the containers, I start losing track of which databank I scanned and which one I am approaching.

This is a very simple mistake.

In a calm situation, it looks small.

But inside a Ghost Site, where time matters, this mistake creates confusion very quickly.

I even lose track of the fourth databank.

So the lesson is clear: tag the containers, or at least keep your target order under control.
