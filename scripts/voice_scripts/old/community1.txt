To participate in Sansha Incursions, a pilot first needs to choose a suitable incursion community.

Each community has its own fleet formats, doctrine requirements, accepted ship fits, language, time zone, and in-game channel.

Before buying an expensive ship, always check the current requirements of the community you want to fly with.

Here is the list of the most relevant public incursion communities for new and returning pilots.

EVE Rookies Incursions is a beginner-friendly public incursion community and a good place to learn the basics.

Warp To Me Incursions is a large public incursion community with established doctrines and multiple fleet formats.

TLA Incursions is a public incursion community focused mainly on advanced Headquarters fleets.

Better Incursion Fleet offers public incursion fleets with Vanguard and Headquarters options.

New Galaxy Age is a Russian-speaking incursion community, mainly active in the EU time zone.

In this guide, we will focus on two common fleet formats: Vanguard fleets and Headquarters fleets.
