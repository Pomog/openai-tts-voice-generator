After landing in null sec, the first rule is: stop and collect information.

Do not immediately warp to a gate.

Do not immediately start scanning like nothing can happen.

Open local chat. Make sure D-scan is visible. Check who is in the system.

If local is empty, good, but that does not mean you are completely safe.

If someone appears in local, check the character. Look at their corporation, alliance, killboard activity, and recent losses or kills.

For example, if a neutral pilot appears, I do not panic immediately.

I check zKillboard and try to estimate the threat.

Is this character active in PvP?

Does he kill explorers?

Does he fly fast tackle, interdictors, or combat ships?

Based on this information, I decide if the threat is critical or not.

Sometimes the correct decision is to leave.

Sometimes the threat is low enough, and I can continue exploring.
