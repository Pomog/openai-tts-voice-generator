Want to join Incursion fleets in EVE Online, but you do not know where to start?

Then New Eden Lab may be the place for you is a beginner-focused corporation created to help new and returning pilots prepare for Incursions.

Here you can find help with basic settings, overview setup, broadcast settings, skill plans, doctrine fits, and choosing your first starter Incursion ship.

The goal is to help you become ready to join them properly.

Incursions can be one of the most stable ways to earn serious ISK in EVE Online.

But for a new player, the hardest part is not only buying the ship.

The hard part is understanding what to train, which fit to use, which community to join, and how to avoid expensive mistakes.

That is exactly what New Eden Lab Academy is focused on.
