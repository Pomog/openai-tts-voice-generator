Capsuleer Day XXIII added Warpath exploration sites to EVE Online.

In this video, I scan down Warpath Intelligence data sites, hack Republic Intelligence Databanks, and test how well my Loki works for this event.

These sites are especially useful in high-sec because the main value is not only the loot from one container. Each completed hack helps with event progress, and that progress leads to seasonal rewards.

You can also collect Warpath event items and use event rewards through the Capsuleer Day system.
