Before committing to exploration, I use the in-game map statistics.

I check activity in the region.

How many pilots are in space?

How many ships were destroyed recently?

Are there many jumps?

Are there NPC kills?

This information helps me understand if the area is quiet, active, or dangerous.

If the region looks too hot, I can simply wait and use another filament later.

But if the area looks acceptable, I plan a route several systems ahead.

Do not move randomly from gate to gate.

Plan your next few jumps.

Check local in every system.

Use D-scan.

Make safe spots.

And never warp directly to a stargate without some kind of reconnaissance.

In hostile space, route planning is part of your tank.
