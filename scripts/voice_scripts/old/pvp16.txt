In this system, I immediately see a six-box Gnosis multiboxer.

And the names in local chat look very strange.

I do not like this situation.

Maybe it is harmless.
Maybe it is just somebody farming sites.
But for a solo Rupture, this is not the kind of system where I want to make random decisions.

So I decide to move on.

But I will not warp directly from gate to gate.

First, I do not know how much damage a smartbombing fleet can apply to a cruiser like this.

Second, there can always be a gatecamp on the next gate.

So before committing the ship, I need reconnaissance.

The safer plan is to bounce first, check the gate with D-scan, look for ships, drones, bubbles, or anything suspicious, and only then decide if the jump is worth the risk.

This is still a PvP roam, but I do not want to feed the Rupture for free.

So first — check the gate.
Then — jump.
