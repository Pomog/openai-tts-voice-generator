Here I found a Warpath strategic site.

For me, these sites are not always worth doing in a hostile environment.

If you already completed the event task line and collected the main rewards, the risk can be higher than the value.

Especially in null sec, every minute inside a site increases the chance that someone finds you.

So before starting the site, ask yourself: is this really worth the risk?

If the reward is low and the system is not safe, I would rather move on and look for better exploration targets.
