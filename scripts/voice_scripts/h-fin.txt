So my hauling checklist is simple.

First, check the cargo value.

Second, check the normal route.

Third, check Gatecheck for camps and gank activity.

Fourth, check EvE-Scout if a Thera or Turnur shortcut makes sense.

Fifth, use a scout when the cargo is valuable.

Sixth, never warp gate to gate in dangerous space without checking.

Seventh, use safe spots, D-scan, and station bookmarks.

And finally, do not be afraid to wait.

In EVE Online, the safest route is not always the shortest route.

The safest route is the route you checked properly.

Fly safe, and do not donate your cargo to the first gate camp on the way to Jita.
