So this was the full high-sec industry experiment.

I started with CONCORD loyalty points, bought blueprints and implants, manufactured ten CONCORD capital modules in high-sec, moved everything to Jita, listed the orders, adjusted prices, and waited for real market transactions.

For me, the main point is not only CONCORD LP conversion.

The interesting part is that this was done through high-sec manufacturing.

No null-sec structures, no low-sec reactions, no risky production chain.

Just blueprints, components, NPC station industry, hauling, market orders, and price management.

The spreadsheet showed one result.

The market showed the real result.

The shield boosters looked stronger as an LP-to-ISK conversion.

The armor repairers had higher sale value, but lower ISK per loyalty point.

The implants were easier to list, but not always better than manufacturing.

And after three days, almost everything was sold, with only one RL-1006 implant still waiting on the market.
