On the undock, we already see some suspicious seagulls around the station.

I do not know who they are exactly.

Maybe scouts, maybe gankers, maybe just people waiting for someone careless.

But honestly, this is not a surprise.

We are only three jumps from Jita, and the closer you get to the biggest trade hub in New Eden, the more strange things you see on grid.

During the trip, I also do a few Project Discovery tasks.

It is not huge money, but it gives some extra ISK and useful bonuses while you are travelling.

If the route is safe and there is nothing important to click, why not make a little extra on the way?

And now we arrive in Jita — the main trade hub of EVE Online.
