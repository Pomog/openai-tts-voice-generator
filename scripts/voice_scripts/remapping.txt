Proper skill remapping is very important.

For example, the NGA Hyperion shield skill queue takes about 52 days and 17 hours without remapping.

With correct remapping, it goes down to about 49 days and 13 hours.

With inexpensive training implants, it can be reduced even further — to around 46 days and 7 hours.

And if you also use the 1 million skill points from the recruitment link, the full training path can take only a few days.

So before you buy an expensive battleship, choose the community first, copy the correct doctrine fit, check the skill queue, and optimize your training plan.

That will save you time, ISK, and mistakes.
