To activate a filament, there are a few important conditions.

First, you need to be in a fleet, even if you are alone.

Second, your safety settings must allow the action.

Third, your cloak must be deactivated.

So before pressing the filament, check your fleet, check your safety, and make sure you are not cloaked.

After activation, the filament will move you into a pseudo-random null sec system.

This is the moment where the real run begins.

You do not know exactly where you will land, who lives there, or how active the area is.

So do not rush.
