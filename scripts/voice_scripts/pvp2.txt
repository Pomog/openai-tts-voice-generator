Before undocking, I prepared three identical Ruptures.

The full fit is in the video description, but the idea is simple:
autocannons, scram, small neut, two cap boosters, and triple armor repair.

And because this is my first real solo PvP attempt, every ship must be insured.
Not because I am pessimistic — but because I am realistic.
I will probably lose all of them.

The Ruptures were transported unassembled to Iitanmadan.
This system will be my starting point, my reship point, and my safe place to reset after each fight.

To find PvP, I will roam through Guristas insurgency systems.
The plan is to move in a cycle, check every system on the route, look for possible targets, and then return back to Iitanmadan.

So this is the experiment:
three Ruptures, one pilot, no real PvP experience, and a simple question —
can I find a fight before New Eden finds me?
