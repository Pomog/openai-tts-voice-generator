After choosing your ship, join the in-game channels of Incursion communities.

Good options for new players are:

EVE Rookies Incursions
Warp To Me Incursions
New Galaxy Age
Better Incursion Fleet

There are other communities too, but in my opinion, these are among the most newbro-friendly places to start learning Incursions.

Join their channels, read their fitting requirements, check their skill plans, and ask questions before buying the ship.
