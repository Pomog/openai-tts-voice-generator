Then I see the worst thing for a solo explorer.

My ship is warp disrupted.

That means I cannot warp out.

If this was a warp scrambler, it would be even worse, because a scrambler would also shut down my MWD.

But this is a disruptor.

So in a desperate attempt to save the ship, I overheat my Multispectrum Shield Hardener and my MWD.

The plan is simple: burn away, get out of disruptor range, and warp out before the ship dies.

This is not a comfortable situation.
