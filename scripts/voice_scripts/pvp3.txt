This Rupture is built for close-range brawling.

Four autocannons provide the damage, with different ammo types for different targets.
The scram keeps enemies from escaping, and the small neut pressures their capacitor.

The core of the fit is triple armor repair: two medium armor repairers and one ancillary rep.
Two cap boosters keep everything alive under pressure.

This Rupture is not fast, but it is stubborn.
