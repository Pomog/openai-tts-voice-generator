Here is our example dataset: two, four, and six.

Written as a vector, these three observations become its three coordinates.

Now we want to separate this vector into two parts.

The first part will represent the common level of the data.

The second part will represent how each observation differs from that common level.

We will call them the mean component and the deviation component.

In other words, the original dataset vector equals the mean component plus the deviation component.
