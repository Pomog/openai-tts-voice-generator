I learned this lesson the expensive way.

This was my Loki, worth almost nine hundred million ISK, and it died inside an AEGIS Capital Ship Security Facility.

Not to players.
Not to rats.
To an AEGIS proximity mine.

My mistake was simple: I treated this site like normal exploration.

Do not do that.

Before moving, zoom out and check the minefield. Do not burn straight to the can. Do not panic-click.

If you move under cloak, be fast: deactivate cloak, press MWD, then reactivate cloak as soon as possible. Reduce the time when you can be locked or targeted.

Move slowly. Watch your path. Keep cloak timing under control.

If the route looks bad, leave. Losing the site is cheaper than losing the ship.

AEGIS sites can pay, but they punish careless flying immediately.

Fly safe.
