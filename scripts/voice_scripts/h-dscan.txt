I use safe spots.

From a safe spot, I can use directional scanner to check the destination gate before I warp there.

D-scan helps you see ships, probes, structures, and other objects within range and angle, so it is one of the most important survival tools in dangerous space.

For example, if I see many combat ships on the next gate, I do not warp my hauler there.

If I see combat scanner probes while I am sitting in a safe spot, I move.

A safe spot is not a permanent home.

It is only a temporary position that gives you time to think.

The moment someone starts hunting you, you should already have the next safe spot ready.
