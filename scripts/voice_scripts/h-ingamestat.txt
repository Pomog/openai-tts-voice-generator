Before I commit to the route, I also check the in-game map.

I look for recent ship kills.

I look for pod kills.

I look for systems with unusual activity.

If one system is hot, I ask myself:

Can I wait?

Can I reroute?

Can I use a wormhole?

Can I split the cargo into smaller loads?

The map will not tell you everything.

But it can show you where people are dying.

And if people are dying on your route right now, you should treat that as a warning.

A patient hauler is usually a richer hauler.

A rushed hauler becomes someone else’s killmail.
