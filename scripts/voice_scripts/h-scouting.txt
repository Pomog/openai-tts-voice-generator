If I am outside high-sec, I do not warp gate to gate with valuable cargo.

That is one of the fastest ways to lose a ship.

The correct method is scouting.

Use an alt, a corpmate, or a cheap fast ship.

The scout jumps first.

The scout checks the gate.

The hauler waits.

If the gate is clear, the hauler moves.

If the gate is camped, the hauler stays safe.

And if I do not have a scout, I reduce the cargo value or I do not move.

This is the hard truth of hauling:

If the cargo is expensive enough, scouting is not optional.

It is part of the cost of transport.
