Another common mistake is ignoring docking and undocking.

You can survive the whole route and still die at the station.

That is why I use instant-dock and instant-undock bookmarks.

An instant-dock bookmark helps you land inside docking range, instead of landing outside and getting bumped or tackled.

An instant-undock bookmark lets you warp immediately after undocking, without slow aligning in front of the station.

This is especially important in trade hubs, low-sec stations, and any place where people wait for haulers.

Do not move expensive cargo from a station you have never bookmarked.

Before hauling, prepare the route.

Prepare the gates.

And prepare the stations.

Hauling safety starts before the cargo is even inside the ship.
