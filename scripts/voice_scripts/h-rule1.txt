The first rule is simple.

Do not use the same method for cheap cargo and expensive cargo.

If I move low-value stuff, I can accept more risk. I can use a normal route, check the dangerous systems, and move quickly.

But if I move expensive cargo, I slow down.

I check alternative routes.

I use scouts.

I use safe bookmarks.

And sometimes, I do not move at all.

This is important: the best hauling decision is sometimes to wait.

If the route is camped, if the map shows recent kills, or if Gatecheck shows gank activity, there is no shame in docking up.

Your cargo does not lose value because you waited.

But it can disappear in one bad jump.
